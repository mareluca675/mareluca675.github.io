VOCABULARY LEARNING APP - ENHANCEMENT IDEAS
===========================================

1. SPACED REPETITION SYSTEM
   - Implement algorithm (like Anki) that shows words at increasing intervals
   - Words you struggle with appear more frequently
   - Words you know well appear less often
   - Automatic scheduling based on performance

2. PRONUNCIATION FEATURES
   - Audio pronunciation for each word (text-to-speech or recordings)
   - Practice pronunciation with microphone input
   - IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet) notation
   - Word stress indicators
   - Syllable breakdown

3. EXAMPLE SENTENCES
   - Add 2-3 example sentences for each vocabulary item
   - Show words in context from real-world usage
   - Sentences from different contexts (formal, informal, academic)
   - Ability to create your own example sentences

4. SYNONYMS & ANTONYMS
   - Show related words for each vocabulary item
   - Expand vocabulary through word relationships
   - Visual word webs or mind maps
   - Similar words that are commonly confused

5. WORD FAMILIES & ETYMOLOGY
   - Show root words, prefixes, and suffixes
   - Word origin and history
   - Related words from same root
   - Language of origin (Latin, Greek, French, etc.)

6. COLLOCATIONS
   - Show common word combinations
   - Which words go together naturally
   - Example: "heavy traffic" not "thick traffic"
   - Preposition usage patterns

7. DAILY CHALLENGES
   - Word of the day feature
   - Daily quiz with 5-10 words
   - Streak tracking (consecutive days studied)
   - Weekly goals and achievements

8. PROGRESS TRACKING & STATISTICS
   - Detailed analytics dashboard
   - Words mastered vs. words to review
   - Time spent studying
   - Performance trends over time
   - Category-wise progress
   - Weak areas identification

9. ADAPTIVE DIFFICULTY
   - System adjusts difficulty based on performance
   - Start with easier words, progress to harder ones
   - Personalized learning path
   - Skip words you already know

10. CUSTOM WORD LISTS
    - Create your own lists (e.g., "Business English", "Travel")
    - Import words from external sources
    - Share lists with others
    - Tag words with custom categories

11. REVIEW REMINDERS
    - Push notifications for review time
    - Email reminders
    - Calendar integration
    - Smart scheduling based on your study patterns

12. GAMIFICATION ELEMENTS
    - Achievement badges and trophies
    - Experience points (XP) system
    - Leaderboards (if multi-user)
    - Level progression
    - Unlockable content

13. MULTIPLE CHOICE VARIATIONS
    - Choose the correct definition
    - Choose the word that matches the definition
    - Choose the correct usage in a sentence
    - Choose synonyms/antonyms

14. WORD SEARCH PUZZLES
    - Find vocabulary words in a grid
    - Themed puzzles (e.g., all idioms, all phrasal verbs)
    - Difficulty levels (easy, medium, hard)
    - Timer mode for speed challenges

15. CROSSWORD PUZZLES
    - Create crosswords using vocabulary words
    - Clues based on definitions or examples
    - Daily crossword challenge
    - Custom crossword generation

16. WORD ASSOCIATION GAMES
    - Connect related words
    - Group words by theme or category
    - Find the odd one out
    - Word chain games

17. SENTENCE COMPLETION EXERCISES
    - Fill in the blank in sentences
    - Multiple blanks in one sentence
    - Choose the correct word form
    - Grammar integration (tenses, articles, etc.)

18. LISTENING COMPREHENSION
    - Audio sentences with vocabulary words
    - Listen and identify the word
    - Dictation exercises
    - Podcast-style learning content

19. WRITING PRACTICE
    - Write sentences using vocabulary words
    - Short paragraph writing with multiple words
    - Essay prompts using specific vocabulary
    - Peer review system (if social features added)

20. VISUAL LEARNING AIDS
    - Images associated with words
    - Mind maps and visual organizers
    - Color coding by category
    - Icon-based memory aids

21. MNEMONIC DEVICES
    - Memory tricks and techniques
    - User-submitted mnemonics
    - Acronyms and wordplay
    - Story-based memorization

22. COMMON MISTAKES
    - Highlight frequently confused words
    - Show what NOT to say
    - Common errors and corrections
    - False friends (words that look similar but mean different)

23. CONTEXTUAL LEARNING
    - Learn words through reading passages
    - Vocabulary in news articles
    - Vocabulary in song lyrics
    - Vocabulary in movie quotes

24. SPEED CHALLENGES
    - Timed flashcard sessions
    - How many words can you review in 5 minutes?
    - Speed quiz mode
    - Reaction time tracking

25. REVERSE LEARNING
    - Start with definition, guess the word
    - Start with example, identify the word
    - Multiple ways to test knowledge
    - Bidirectional learning

26. WORD FREQUENCY INDICATORS
    - Mark words by usage frequency (common, rare, etc.)

    - Academic word list integration
    - Business English word list

27. CULTURAL CONTEXT
