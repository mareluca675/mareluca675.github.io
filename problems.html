The Problem

Human–bear conflicts, late detection, and lack of timely alerts increase risks for both.

Key Challenges

  • Late detection in populated areas and trails
  • No unified alerting mechanism for rangers and citizens
  • Limited connectivity in mountain regions
  • High false positives from generic motion detection

Impact

  • Increased risk of dangerous human–bear encounters
  • Reactive rather than preventive interventions
  • Negative outcomes for wildlife conservation
