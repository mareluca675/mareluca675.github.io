Troubleshooting

No bears detected?

  • Lower confidence threshold: --confidence 0.3
  • Ensure good lighting and test with clear images
  • Try a larger model: --model yolov8x.pt

Too slow?

  • Use a smaller model: --model yolov8n.pt
  • Check that GPU is enabled
  • Reduce video resolution or process every nth frame

Camera not working?

  • Try a different camera index: --source 1 or --source 2
  • Check camera permissions
  • Test with a video file instead
