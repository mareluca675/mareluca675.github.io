Our Team

Experts in wildlife conservation, AI technology, and community engagement working together to protect Romania's wildlife

Who We Are

We're a multidisciplinary team of wildlife biologists, AI researchers, software engineers, and conservation experts dedicated to creating innovative solutions for human-wildlife coexistence in Romania's Carpathian Mountains. Our diverse expertise allows us to approach conservation challenges from multiple angles, combining cutting-edge technology with deep ecological knowledge and community engagement.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Mission-Driven

Every decision we make prioritizes wildlife conservation and human safety equally. We measure success not in profits, but in lives protected and ecosystems preserved.

Compassion

We believe in coexistence—respecting both human needs and animal welfare. Our solutions seek harmony, not dominance over nature.

Excellence

Scientific rigor and cutting-edge technology drive everything we do. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of research and implementation.

Community

Local communities are our partners in conservation, not obstacles. We build solutions with people, not for them.
