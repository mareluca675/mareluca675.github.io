How it works

The system captures frames from a camera or video file, resizes them to 640Ã—640, runs YOLO once per frame, keeps only detections where the class is bear above a confidence threshold, and draws bounding boxes with confidence scores.

  1. Input: Camera or video file
  2. Detection: YOLO processes each frame
  3. Filtering: Keep bear above threshold
  4. Output: Boxes + confidence overlaid
