Our Mission

Protecting Romania's wildlife through innovative AI technology

Smart Conservation for the Carpathians

Romania is home to Europe's largest brown bear population—over 6,000 individuals living in the Carpathian Mountains. As human activities expand into these wilderness areas, the need for intelligent, real-time monitoring has never been greater.

BearAware uses advanced AI to detect bears instantly, alerting communities and protecting both people and wildlife. Our system runs 24/7, providing critical data for conservation efforts while keeping hikers and residents safe.
